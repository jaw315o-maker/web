COMPANY PROFILE

Leadway was established in year 2006.

Leadway specialized in India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Southeast Asia, and Africa markets and have technicians who have more than 15-year machine installation experiences to provide on-site and online customer services.

Our general manager, Mr. Nag, began his career in footwear machinery in 1968, providing molds, machine plans, production process design, technical know-how and raw materials. Before coming to Taiwan, his job in India was importing Italian and German shoemaking machines.

He visited Taiwan for looking for Taiwanese machine and with his expertise in EVA and PVC mold design and production, he made more than 100 molds during his 1st time visit about 2 1/2 months staying in Taiwan. Mr. Nag also invented and owned a patent for PVC three-color DIP molds.

We have the most advanced machines for making EVA slippers, sandals, sport shoes and EVA recycling systems. We also have strong team specialized in PVC and Rubber machinery.

India and Bangladesh are speedy growing countries. There are also many fast-developing countries in Africa. We hope that we could help and would like to share our Taiwanese industries experiences with our clients.

Our company mission is to provide turn-key services and to assist successful local business in order to provide many more people in their countries to have good job opportunities. It has always been our wish to make this world better and to protect our environment.



公司簡介

Leadway 成立於2006年

Leadway專注於印度、孟加拉、斯里蘭卡、東南亞、非洲市場，擁有15年以上機器安裝經驗的技術人員，提供現場或在線客戶服務。

我們的職業經理納格先生於1968年開始從事製鞋機械，提供模具、整機計劃、生產工藝設計、技術訣竅和原材料。來台灣之前，他在印度的工作是進口意大利和德國的製鞋機。 Nag先生第一次來台灣2 1/2個月，在這短短的時間裡，憑藉對EVA和PVC模具設計和生產的專業知識，他製作了100多個模具。

我們擁有最先進的 EVA 鞋類生產機器和 EVA 回收系統。我們還擁有強大的 PVC 和橡膠機械團隊。

印度和孟加拉國是快速發展的國家。非洲也有許多快速發展的國家。我們希望能幫到您，並願意與您分享我們台灣工業的經驗。

我們的使命是提供交鑰匙服務並協助成功的企業，以便為您所在國家/地區的更多人提供良好的工作機會。讓這個世界變得更美好並保護我們的環境一直是我們的願望。
